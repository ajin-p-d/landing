WELCOME TO NOSCE

To be successful in this increasingly competitive world, one should be well aware about oneself. Self awareness is all about knowing your emotions, personal strengths & weaknesses and having a sense of self worth.

NOSCE is a Personal Enrichment & Support Team. We have a team of self motivated and professionally qualified members to support you in your pursuit of knowing yourself. We ought to nourish your talents and build your confidence so that you can face the day today challenges successfully.

We offer campus based soft skill and placement orientation to graduate and post graduate students. Ongoing placement assistance and technical aptitude training would be an added advantage. Also, we work with corporates & provide customized training to employees based on their projected needs. We are blessed with a wide range of satisfied customers, both from educational and corporate sectors whose feedback and support drives us forward.

Hoping to serve you more & better.

TESTIMONIALS

Bhavan’s College Of Arts and Commerce, Kakkanad via their Women’s cell conducted a session in stress and relationship management on 17th July, 2019. The session presided by Ms. Besi Paul, Chief Mentor and CEO of Nosce, was rich in knowledge about the dilemmas and relationship pressures the current adolescent generation is facing. It addressed the various issues in the society detrimental to the women in general and solutions for the same given. It had been an enlightening session coupled with fun anecdotes helping to nurture a positive attitude in the minds of students and look at things from a wider angle.

Thank you NOSCE team for this wonderful session.

Women Cell – Bavans Arts & Commerce, College
